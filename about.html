What we do and How we do it

In the recent times, the domain of Artificial Intelligence(AI) is most talked about in technology. It has seen great advances in near present. Our web application is the humblest possible traversal into that world. We call it Machine Learning(ML). We enumerate the percentage chances of upcoming movies to get scaled as a hit. We are able to do it from previous data like budget of the film, caste and crew and name of the film. We feed this data to the computer and computer(a machine) learns from this data how to classify any new data it encounters and so we are able to predict rise and fall.

Vision & Mission

Think of a situation. You have been caught stealing candles by a police officer. He takes you in his custody and you beg for mercy. Then he receives a call from his wife who has become very bored. You overhear their conversation and the open our application. You recommend a film to the officer and his wife. Both enjoy the film and your life is saved. We want to save your life. That is our sole purpose.
